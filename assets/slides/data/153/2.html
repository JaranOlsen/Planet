Enneatype 2w1

The Servant

  • Growth recommendations:
    • Develop a habit of self-reflection to recognize your own needs and feelings.
    • Learn to ask for help and express your needs to others without feeling guilty.
    • Cultivate an understanding that you can be loved for who you are, not just for what you do for others.
    • Allow yourself to accept that you're not responsible for everyone else's happiness.
    • Seek to balance your inner ideals with the realities and limitations of the world.
