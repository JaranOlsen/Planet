The Bohemian

  • Growth recommendations:
    • Learn to manage feelings of melancholy and over-identification with your emotions.
    • Develop strategies to engage more actively with the world around you.
    • Value the present moment and the concrete world as much as your inner world.
    • Work on understanding and expressing your needs in a constructive way.
    • Accept that it's okay to be ordinary and still be valuable and lovable.
