Enneatype 7

The Enthusiast

  • Healthy Levels:
    1. At their best, Type 7 individuals assimilate experiences in depth, which makes them deeply grateful and appreciative for what they have. They become awed by the simple wonders of life and exhibit joyous and ecstatic feelings. They may also experience intimations of spiritual reality and the boundless goodness of life.

    3. They easily become accomplished achievers and generalists who excel at many different things: multi-talented. They are practical, productive, usually prolific, and capable of cross-fertilizing areas of interest.
