The Defender

The Loyal, Analytical Type: Responsible, Engaged, Alert, Private, and Intellectual.

As a Six wing Five, you blend the loyal, responsible, and security-oriented nature of a Six with the introspective, intellectual traits of a Five. This combination often results in a deeply committed and thoughtful individual who seeks understanding and stability in their world. Your desire for security and loyalty is balanced by a keen intellectual curiosity and a desire for personal autonomy.

Your Five wing can make you more introspective and independent than many Sixes, driving you to seek knowledge and understanding as a means of dealing with uncertainty. You're likely to use your intellectual capabilities to dissect problems and ensure safety and stability for yourself and your community.
