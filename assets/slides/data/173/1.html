Enneatype 2

The Helper

  • Healthy Levels:
    1. At their best, Type 2 individuals embody selflessness, humility, and genuine altruism. They give love unconditionally and feel privileged to be a part of others' lives.
    2. They are empathetic and compassionate, often prioritizing the needs of others. Their warmth, sincerity, and capacity for forgiveness make them approachable and dependable.
    3. They uplift and appreciate those around them, seeing the goodness in people. While they are committed to serving others, they also understand the importance of self-care. They are nurturing, generous, and genuinely loving individuals.
