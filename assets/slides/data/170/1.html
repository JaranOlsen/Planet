Enneatype 1

The Reformer

  • Healthy Levels:
    1. At their best, Type 1 individuals are the epitome of wisdom and acceptance. They are realistically attuned to the world and are skilled at recognizing the best course of action in any situation. They become beacons of hope and truth, inspiring others around them.
    2. With a strong sense of personal responsibility, they act with a clear moral compass. They are the embodiment of rationality, self-discipline, and strive to be the adults they think they should be. Their balance and integrity make them reliable and trustworthy.
    3. They radiate fairness, objectivity, and dedication to ethical conduct. Their strong sense of purpose and commitment to their principles often inspire them to educate others, becoming influential role models of truth and justice.
