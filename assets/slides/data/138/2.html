Scientific findings

  • Neuroplasticity: Consistent meditation practice leads to measurable changes in brain structure, enhancing self-awareness and attention.
  • Anxiety Reduction: Meditation has been found effective in mitigating stress and anxiety by promoting mindfulness and detachment.
  • Telomere Length: Studies suggest meditation may slow cellular aging by influencing the length of telomeres, protective elements of chromosomes.
  • Cardiovascular Benefits: Regular meditation can positively affect heart health, reducing heart rate, blood pressure, and cholesterol levels.
