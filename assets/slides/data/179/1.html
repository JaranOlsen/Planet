Enneatype 4

The Individualist

  • Healthy Levels:
    1. At their best, Type 4 individuals are deeply creative, capable of transforming personal and universal experiences into valuable creations, possibly even works of art. They are inspired, continuously renewing themselves.
    2. These individuals are self-aware and introspective, always on a quest for self-discovery. They are attuned to their feelings and impulses and show sensitivity and intuition towards both self and others.
    3. They embrace their uniqueness and stay true to themselves. They are open about their emotions, humane, and possess a unique mix of vulnerability and emotional strength, often communicated through a blend of seriousness and humor.
