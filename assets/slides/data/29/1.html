Basic Method of EFT

  • Context:
    • Interaction with a therapist
    • Ideally a safe space
    • Ideally a loving relation
  • Important issues evoked by:
    • Talking about life events and inner experiences
    • Important feelings and experiences emerging in a safe space / loving relation
    • The guidance of the therapist
  • Method of healing / integration:
    • Changing emotion with emotion
    • Progressive processing of layers of emotion
    • Experience of being seen, heard, and loved
