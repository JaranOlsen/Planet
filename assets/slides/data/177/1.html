Enneatype 3

The Achiever

  • Average Levels:
    1. Performance becomes a key focus, as they relentlessly drive towards their goals. Their self-worth becomes intertwined with their achievements. A fear of failure spurs them on, and they begin to gauge their status through comparisons with others.
    2. Image-consciousness emerges, and they start to shape themselves based on others' expectations. Pragmatic and efficient, they risk losing touch with their feelings, leading to potential issues with authenticity and intimacy.
    3. They seek to impress others with their perceived superiority, often promoting themselves excessively. Narcissistic tendencies may arise, with grandiose self-views. Their seductive exhibitionism is a cover for underlying jealousy and insecurity.
