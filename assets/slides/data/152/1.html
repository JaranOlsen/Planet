Enneatype 1w9

The Idealist

The Rational, Idealistic, Peaceful Type: Principled, Purposeful, Self-Controlled, Perfectionistic, Patient, and Accepting.

As a One wing Nine, you embody many of the characteristics of Enneatype 1, including a deep sense of responsibility, integrity, and adherence to ethical and moral standards. Your strong inner critic persists, encouraging you towards perfection and order. However, the influence of the Nine wing introduces a more patient, accepting and serene aspect to your personality.

While you're still bothered by imperfections and inefficiencies, you're often more able to let go, prioritize peace, and accept things as they are. Your approach to teaching and improving others' lives is tempered by an understanding and respect for their individual paths and rhythms. This unique blend makes you a balanced force for positive change.
