Empathy as the Overarching Quality

  • Empathy: A cornerstone of the humanistic, client-centered approach, fostering therapeutic alliance and empowering client's self-healing.
  • Facilitates experiential processing of emotions, crucial for therapeutic change.
  • Enables the therapist to grasp the client's felt experience, fostering authentic relational depth.
"Empathy is the therapist's capacity to understand consciously, immediately, or retrospectively the patient's inner world, as if the therapist were the patient" - Heinz Kohut, The Restoration of the Self (1977)
