The Enthusiast

  • Average Levels:
    1. As their restlessness increases, they seek more options and choices. They become adventurous and "worldly wise," but less focused, constantly seeking new things and experiences. They value money, variety, and keeping up with the latest trends.
    2. They struggle to discriminate what they truly need, become hyperactive, and unable to say "no" to themselves. They immerse themselves in constant activity. Uninhibited, they do and say whatever comes to mind: storytelling, flamboyant exaggerations, witty wise-cracking, and performing.
