Enneatype 4

The Individualist

  • Unhealthy Levels:
    1. Failure of dreams leads to self-inhibition, self-anger, depression, and alienation. They might feel ashamed and fatigued, unable to function effectively.
    2. They might become tormented by extreme self-contempt, blaming others and pushing away anyone who tries to help. They become a source of their own torment.
    3. In despair and feeling hopeless, they might resort to self-destructive behaviors such as substance abuse. In extreme cases, they are prone to emotional breakdowns or suicide. This level generally corresponds to severe Avoidant, Depressive, and Narcissistic personality disorders.
