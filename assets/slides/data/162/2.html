The Hostess

  • Growth recommendations:
    • Cultivate genuine self-esteem from within, independent of external validation.
    • Practice mindful self-reflection to recognize when you're adjusting yourself to please others.
    • Learn to set boundaries and express your own needs and feelings openly.
    • Appreciate the value of authentic relationships over impressing or winning over others.
    • Accept that it's okay to put your needs first sometimes.
