The Investigator

  • Unhealthy Levels:
    1. They become reclusive and isolated from reality, eccentric and nihilistic. Highly unstable and fearful of aggression, they reject and repel others and all social connections.
    2. They become obsessed and frightened by their own threatening ideas, leading to horror, delirium, distortions, and phobias.
    3. In their quest for oblivion, they may commit suicide or suffer a psychotic break with reality. They can become deranged, explosively self-destructive, displaying schizophrenic overtones. This level generally corresponds to the Schizoid, Avoidant, and Schizotypal personality disorders.
