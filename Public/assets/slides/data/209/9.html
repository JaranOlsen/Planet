Focusing

The Method

Step 01 · Clearing a Space

Gently sense inside your body and notice the main things in your life right now, acknowledging them without going into them.

Step 02 · Felt Sense

Choose one issue and let yourself feel its unclear, whole bodily sense.

Step 03 · Handle

Let a word, phrase, or image emerge that captures the quality of that felt sense.

Step 04 · Resonating

Go back and forth between the felt sense and the handle until they match and feel right.

Step 05 · Asking

Gently ask the felt sense what makes the problem have this quality, waiting for a fresh bodily response.

Step 06 · Receiving

Welcome whatever comes with a sense of release, staying with it in a friendly way.
