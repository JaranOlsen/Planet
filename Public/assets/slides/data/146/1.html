Enneatype 4

The Individualist

The Sensitive, Withdrawn Type: Expressive, Dramatic, Self-Absorbed, and Temperamental.

As a Four, you are deeply in touch with your own emotions and long for understanding and authenticity. You have a rich inner life, often finding solace and self-expression in art, literature, or other forms of creativity. You feel different from others, and there's a persistent sense of longing for something undefined, something that will give meaning to your life or complete your identity.

At times, you may compare yourself with others, feeling as if they possess qualities that you lack. You value emotional honesty and tend to be more comfortable with melancholy than other types.
