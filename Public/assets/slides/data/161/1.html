Enneatype 1w2

The Advocate

The Rational, Idealistic, Generous Type: Principled, Purposeful, Self-Controlled, Perfectionistic, Caring, and Altruistic.

As a One wing Two, you carry the core traits of the Enneatype 1, such as an unwavering sense of responsibility, integrity, and devotion to ethical standards. Your inner critic remains a driving force towards perfection and orderliness. However, the Two wing adds a layer of empathy, care, and a desire to help others.

You may still find yourself frustrated by imperfections and inefficiencies, but this frustration often extends towards injustices or unfairness faced by others. This mix of dedication to high standards and caring for others often leads you to take action, not just for your own improvement, but for the betterment of your community as well. Your natural teaching instincts may manifest in active mentorship, offering guidance to others not just for practical improvements, but for emotional growth as well.
