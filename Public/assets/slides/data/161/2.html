The Advocate

  • Growth recommendations:
    • Establish boundaries to avoid overcommitting yourself or meddling in others' affairs.
    • Practice self-care and allocate time for your personal needs and relaxation.
    • Regularly reflect on your motivations to ensure they are guided by genuine concern rather than a need to be needed.
    • Appreciate that perfection is an ideal, not a practical expectation.
    • Understand that you can't fix everything and everyone, and that's okay.
