The Enthusiastic, Loyal Type: Spontaneous, Versatile, Fun-loving, Responsible, and Engaged.

As a Seven wing Six, you combine the enthusiastic, spontaneous, and versatile traits of a Seven with the engaged, responsible, and loyal qualities of a Six. You seek excitement and new experiences, but also value stability and community.

Your Six wing adds a layer of loyalty and engagement to your enthusiastic nature, making you a more grounded and practical adventurer.
