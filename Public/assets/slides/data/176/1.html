Enneatype 3

The Achiever

  • Healthy Levels:
    1. At their best, Type 3 individuals exemplify authenticity, acceptance, and a generous spirit. Their humility and charitable nature are accompanied by a delightful sense of humor and a warm heart.
    2. Self-assured and vibrant, they believe in their worth and abilities. They exude a natural charm and grace that make them appealing and likable.
    3. Their ambition is directed towards self-improvement. They strive to embody the best version of themselves, inspiring others through their remarkable effectiveness and admirable qualities.
