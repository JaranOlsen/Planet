Enneatype 6

The Loyalist

The Committed, Security-Oriented Type: Engaging, Responsible, Anxious, and Suspicious.

As a Six, you are driven by a need for security and stability. You have a highly developed "inner radar," enabling you to foresee potential issues and problems before they arise. You're loyal to your values, beliefs, and people, often going to great lengths to uphold these commitments.

Despite being skeptical and cautious, once you form a trust, you're highly dedicated and supportive. However, you might struggle with doubt and anxiety, often second-guessing your decisions and worrying about what might go wrong.
