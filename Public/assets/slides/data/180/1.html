Enneatype 4

The Individualist

  • Average Levels:
    1. They adopt an artistic and romantic approach to life, seeking to prolong personal feelings by creating a beautiful aesthetic environment. They turn to fantasy, passion, and imagination to enhance their reality.
    2. They internalize everything in an effort to stay connected with their feelings, which can lead to self-absorption, mood swings, shyness, and self-consciousness. They might withdraw to safeguard their self-image and take time to understand their feelings.
    3. Over time, they may start to feel different from others, leading to a sense of exemption from normal life. They might become melancholic dreamers, living in a fantasy world, leading to self-pity, envy of others, and increasing impracticality.
