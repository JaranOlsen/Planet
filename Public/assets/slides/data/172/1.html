Enneatype 1

The Reformer

  • Unhealthy Levels:
    1. They can become unyielding, self-righteous, and intolerant, espousing an 'absolute truth' mindset. They may become overly judgmental, while hypocritically justifying their own behaviors.
    2. An unhealthy One may develop an obsessive focus on the faults of others, and despite preaching about virtues, might engage in behaviors that starkly contrast with their stated ideals.
    3. At their worst, Ones can become punishing, cruel, and condemnatory. They may suffer from severe depression and anxiety, and in extreme cases, there may be a risk of self-harm or suicide. This stage often aligns with Obsessive-Compulsive and Depressive personality disorders.
