Enneatype 1

The Reformer

The Rational, Idealistic Type: Principled, Purposeful, Self-Controlled, and Perfectionistic.

As a One, you are defined by your sense of responsibility, integrity, and the commitment to moral and ethical standards. You have an inner critic, a ceaseless voice that strives for perfection and order. This inner critic can be both a source of great discipline and a catalyst for stress, as it drives you to avoid mistakes and faults at all costs.

You might find yourself frequently irritated by imperfections or inefficiencies, not just in your own actions, but in the world around you. You're a natural teacher, often striving to impart your wisdom and methods to others to improve not just your life but theirs as well.
