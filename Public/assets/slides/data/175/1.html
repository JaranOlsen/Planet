Enneatype 2

The Helper

  • Unhealthy Levels:
    1. Type 2 individuals can become manipulative, using guilt to extract favors and sympathy from others. They may abuse food and medication as a coping mechanism, and can undermine and belittle people, while denying their aggressive or selfish motives.
    2. They can become demanding and coercive, feeling entitled to repayment from others in various forms, be it monetary or even in the form of sexual favors.
    3. At their worst, they may rationalize their actions, feel victimized, and harbor deep resentment. This suppressed aggression might manifest as chronic health issues, burdening others with their problems. This stage often aligns with Histrionic Personality Disorder and Factitious Disorder.
