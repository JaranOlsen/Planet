Enneatype 5

The Investigator

  • Healthy Levels:
    1. At their best, Type 5 individuals are visionary, comprehending the world broadly yet profoundly. Open-minded, they perceive things as a whole in their true context. They're capable of making pioneering discoveries and seeing new ways of understanding and doing things.
    2. They possess extraordinary perceptiveness and insight, with mental alertness that misses nothing. They exhibit foresight and prediction, and have the ability to concentrate and engross themselves in whatever captures their interest.
    3. They achieve skillful mastery in their areas of interest. Driven by the excitement of knowledge, they often become experts in a field, and their innovative, inventive minds produce valuable original works. They are highly independent, idiosyncratic, and whimsical.
