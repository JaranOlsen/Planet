The Dreamer

The Peaceful, Idealistic Type: Easygoing, Accepting, Comfort-seeking, Principled, and Purposeful.

As a Nine wing One, you blend the peace-seeking, accepting, and comfort-oriented nature of a Nine with the principled, purposeful, and perfection-seeking traits of a One. This combination results in a conscientious and serene personality, striving for peace and harmony while also upholding strong ethical standards. Your desire for inner and outer peace is complemented by a strong sense of right and wrong.

Your One wing adds an idealistic and principled layer to your pursuit of peace, leading you to not just seek harmony but to also strive for improvement and perfection. This combination creates a peaceful yet purposeful individual, who values serenity and principles in equal measure.
