Enneatype 9w1

The Dreamer

  • Growth recommendations:
    • Develop the courage to assert your needs and desires.
    • Practice mindfulness to stay connected with your feelings and desires.
    • Balance your desire for peace with the necessity of change and growth.
    • Acknowledge and confront conflicts rather than avoiding them.
    • Cultivate patience with yourself and others, understanding that perfection is unattainable.
