Enneatype 5

The Investigator

  • Average Levels:
    1. They start to conceptualize and fine-tune everything before acting, working things out in their minds through model building, preparation, and practice. They become specialized, often challenging established ways of doing things.
    2. As they get involved with complex ideas or imaginary worlds, they become increasingly detached. Preoccupied with their visions and interpretations, they may lose touch with reality. They show interest in off-beat, esoteric subjects, even those with dark and disturbing elements.
    3. They begin to oppose anything that interferes with their inner world and personal vision, becoming provocative, abrasive, cynical, and argumentative.
